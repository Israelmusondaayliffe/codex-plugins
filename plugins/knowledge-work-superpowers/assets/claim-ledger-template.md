# Claim Ledger

| Claim ID | Claim | Status | Source IDs | Confidence | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | [Specific claim] | [supported, inferred, disputed, or unresolved] | [S1] | [high, medium, or low] | [Reasoning, counterevidence, or limitation] |

Status rules:

- `supported`: the cited evidence directly supports the claim.
- `inferred`: the claim follows from evidence but is not stated directly.
- `disputed`: credible sources conflict.
- `unresolved`: evidence is missing or too weak.

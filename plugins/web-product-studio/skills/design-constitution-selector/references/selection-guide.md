# Design constitution guide

- design-taste-frontend: general high-quality frontend direction when no narrower style is requested.
- gpt-taste: opinionated product design and critique.
- hallmark: anti-generic visual identity and distinctive composition.
- high-end-visual-design: premium art direction and refined visual systems.
- industrial-brutalist-ui: raw, mechanical, high-contrast interfaces.
- minimalist-ui: restrained, quiet, content-first interfaces.
- stitch-design-taste: semantic design language and Stitch-oriented composition.

Use none when a supplied screenshot, established design system, or project contract already defines the visual authority.

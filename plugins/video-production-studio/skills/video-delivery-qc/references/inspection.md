# Video inspection

Inspect at minimum:

1. opening frame and first three seconds.
2. a text-heavy moment for size, contrast, spelling, and safe areas.
3. a transition-heavy moment for dropped frames or abrupt motion.
4. midpoint for visual and audio continuity.
5. final frame and call to action.
6. caption timing against speech at the start, middle, and end.
7. source media for cropping, stretching, color shifts, or missing content.

Record exact timestamps for every failure.

# Style Families

Six style families identified in high-engagement Seedance 2.0 prompts. Use as creative directions when the brief is vague or when exploring variations.

## 1. Cinematic Narrative

Director-style, timeline-scripted, multi-shot storytelling. Emotional beats, character focus, dramatic lighting.

Signature elements: timeline brackets, close-up to wide progression, diegetic dialogue cues, 35mm anamorphic, warm film grain.

Exemplar structure: Racing movie / Japanese pure love short / wuxia duel.

## 2. Action / VFX

High kinetic energy, continuous long takes, combat or stunt sequences, particle effects, environmental destruction.

Signature elements: smooth tracking shot, muzzle flash / impact effects, slow-motion on peaks, aggressive handheld POV, epic score atmosphere.

Exemplar structure: Female warrior in abandoned factory / samurai charge / FPV racing.

## 3. Product / Commercial

Clean, minimal, premium aesthetic. Slow reveals, reflective surfaces, satisfying motion.

Signature elements: orbiting close-up, rack focus to logo, dramatic rim light, glossy materials, subtle impact audio.

Exemplar structure: Smartphone drop-and-reform / headphone campaign / beverage pour.

## 4. Character Portrait in Motion

Intimate, subtle, emotional. Micro-expressions and minimal movement.

Signature elements: slow dolly-in, medium close-up, golden hour rim light, shallow DOF, gentle wind on hair, soft breathing audio.

Exemplar structure: Silver-haired woman on mountain / rooftop terrace turn / vintage portrait.

## 5. Environment / Landscape

Scale and atmosphere. Dynamic natural changes, drone/aerial framing, epic but unhurried.

Signature elements: sweeping aerial drone, volumetric rays, mist rolling in, golden hour transition, ambient nature audio.

Exemplar structure: Misty valley reveal / autumn river / ancient fortress flyover.

## 6. UGC / Meme / Vlog

Handheld, vertical, lo-fi or deliberately playful. Mockumentary or first-person selfie framing.

Signature elements: 9:16 vertical, natural camera shake, film grain filter, exaggerated reactions, meme-style captions, selfie POV.

Exemplar structure: Taiwanese girlfriend POV vlog / Godzilla cat mockumentary / cat knocking objects.

## How to Use

When the user's brief is thin, offer 2-3 style family directions as options. When the brief is clear, pick the matching family and load its signature elements into the prompt's Style, Camera, and Audio lines.

Style families can also be mixed. "Cinematic Narrative meets UGC" produces mockumentary drama. "Product Commercial meets Action VFX" produces a hype reel. Name the fusion explicitly in the Style line.

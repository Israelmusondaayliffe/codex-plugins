# Variation Axes

Use these to avoid shallow option sets:

- Audience: expert, buyer, beginner, skeptic, fan, internal team.
- Tone: direct, editorial, technical, playful, premium, blunt.
- Risk: safe, inventive, bold, experimental.
- Format: checklist, story, framework, memo, prompt, script, table.
- Visual language: documentary, cinematic, diagrammatic, product-led, editorial.
- Business goal: conversion, trust, clarity, speed, differentiation, retention.
- Time horizon: immediate, weekly, quarterly, durable system.
- Constraint posture: minimal, strict, compliance-heavy, creative-first.

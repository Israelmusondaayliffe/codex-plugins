# Brand World routing

brief: turn approved business context into a visual decision brief.
identity: create a brand kit, logo system, or visual-world board.
model-route: choose the image model and production mode for the job.
prompt-pack: create a repeatable set of generation or editing prompts.
verify: compare a series against the approved brand brief and references.

Positioning and commercial strategy stay upstream. Brand World owns visual direction, model routing, prompt production, and consistency checks.

## How to Use This Library

1. **When stuck**: Pick 3 random directions from different categories, combine
2. **For series**: Move systematically through one category
3. **For anti-sameness**: Ensure 10 prompts span at least 5 different categories
4. **For client work**: Match directions to brand personality
5. **For experimentation**: Go to "Experimental Territories" first

## Combination Examples

**Direction 1 + 43 + 63**: Macro intimacy + Anachronistic clash + Beautiful melancholy
→ "Extreme close-up of a tear running down a cheek, but the skin texture is porcelain like an 18th-century automaton, beautiful sadness rendered in clockwork precision"

**Direction 10 + 51 + 91**: Bird's eye + Private jet + Just after
→ "Aerial view of private jet interior, champagne glass tipped over, papers scattered, passenger seat empty—someone just left in a hurry"

**Direction 24 + 39 + 101**: Layered depth + Neon reflection + Fragmented reality
→ "Rain-soaked Tokyo street, neon signs reflecting in puddles, but the reflections are from different cities—Paris, New York, Mumbai—all bleeding together"

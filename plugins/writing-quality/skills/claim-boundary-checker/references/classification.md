# Claim classification

- supported: the supplied or verified evidence directly supports the claim at its stated scope.
- unsupported: no evidence in the allowed set supports the claim.
- uncertain: evidence is incomplete, conflicting, stale, or indirect.
- non-factual: opinion, preference, instruction, or clearly marked projection.

For unsupported or uncertain claims choose one remedy: remove, qualify, verify, or source. Do not widen a source beyond what it establishes.

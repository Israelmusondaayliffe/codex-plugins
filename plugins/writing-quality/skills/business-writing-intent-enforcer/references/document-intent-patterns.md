# Document Intent Patterns

## Decision Memo

Use when the reader must approve, reject, or choose.

Core sections: recommendation, context, options, risks, decision needed.

## Operating Spec

Use when the reader must execute consistently.

Core sections: purpose, rules, workflow, edge cases, acceptance checks.

## Project Plan

Use when the reader must understand sequence and ownership.

Core sections: objective, phases, dependencies, risks, verification.

## Executive Summary

Use when the reader needs fast comprehension.

Core sections: what changed, why it matters, recommended action, open questions.

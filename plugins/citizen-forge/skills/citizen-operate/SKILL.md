# Citizen Operate

Read references/lifecycle.md and references/recovery.md. Verify state, audit chain, ownership, health, usage, review deadline, and recovery evidence.

Missing primary or backup ownership in a shared app routes to TRANSFER_REQUIRED and restricts changes and releases. Inactivity may recommend archive, but never delete the application or its data automatically. Record ownership transfers, archive decisions, recovery events, and retirement in the append-only audit chain.

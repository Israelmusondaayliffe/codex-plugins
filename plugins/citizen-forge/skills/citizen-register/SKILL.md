# Citizen Register

Require a confirmed brief. Prefer a user-selected workspace catalog. Otherwise use the per-project catalog and explain that duplicate detection is limited.

Invoke the deterministic register command. Present likely overlaps with app name, owner, and similarity. A confirmed duplicate routes to reuse. A likely duplicate pauses for owner confirmation. Never provision a duplicate automatically.

# Privacy

The repository and bundled plugins do not collect or transmit personal data by
default. LoopKit stores run contracts, checkpoints, event records, and evidence
references locally under the user's Codex home directory. It does not include
network clients, analytics, telemetry, MCP servers, or app connectors.

Codex, GitHub, optional tools, and external services used with these plugins are
governed by their own privacy policies. Users control the files, accounts,
credentials, connectors, and permissions made available to an active task.

Do not include secrets or sensitive personal information in public issues,
sample contracts, completion receipts, or committed evidence.
